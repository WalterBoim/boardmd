FSRS Spaced Repetition
The Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler — the same algorithm behind Anki. Cards come back at the optimal moment to maximize long-term retention.
Local Persistence
Your progress is saved automatically in the browser. Close the tab, come back tomorrow — your intervals and scores are right where you left them.
Again / Hard / Good / Easy
Rate each card after revealing the answer. Estimated intervals shown before you choose, so you know exactly what you're scheduling.

Drill into a single system — Cardiology, Neurology, Biostatistics — or study all systems together. Filters sync across sidebar and tabs.

A/B/C/D selects your answer. 1/2/3/4 rates the card. Arrow keys navigate. Study without lifting your hands from the keyboard.
